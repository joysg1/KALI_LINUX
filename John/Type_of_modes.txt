++ Single mode
--- john --single <hash.txt> --format=RAW-MD5


++ Worldlist mode
--- john --worldlist=wordlist.txt <hash.txt> --format=RAW-MD5


++ Mask mode
--- john --mask=?d?d?d?d?d?d <hash.txt> --format=RAW-MD5
---- Mask modes:
----- ?d ---> Only digits (0-9)
----- ?l ---> Lowercase letters (a-z)
----- ?u ---> Uppercase letters (A-Z)
----- ?s ---> Special characters
----- ?a ---> Any printable character
